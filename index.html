The problem

Users' Pain point: Hard to find consistent and accurate data sources for exhibition information. Through our research only 10.22% of users are satisfied with exhibition information on the existing platforms. 60% of users feel that the content of the platform is not sufficient and some exhibitions cannot be found on the platform. Only 14.49% felt that the existing search method works well. Without hint or guidance, searching for the right information is not that easy.

Target User Group

The explorers: they have little ideas/preferences about exhibitions,willingness to go (e.g. to kill time).
The discoverer: they have some ideas (e.g. educational events, in general),want to narrow down their choice.
The validators: they know exactly what kind of exhibitions they want to go (e.g. photography), want to find one exhibition.

The solution

MUSE has a large database covering information of most exhibitions and museums. We also have sorted result list: Users can use tags to quickly find the exhibitions with their expected traits. We also focus on efficiency: Tags can also be used to filter the searching results. To provide a better service, we also focus on Community & Engagement. Users can share their reviews of the exhibition and post it in Discover section. The posts in Discover section can also be the reference for other users

Market Research

According to China Art Museum Industry Analysis Report 2019,
the trend shows that the visitor number is increasing.
Since we are an app targeting people visiting exhibition,
this means we have a promising future.
Furthermore, the potential market size is very huge. If we manage
to catch part of this huge market, our app can be profitable.

Personna

Base on our user research, we build three personas represent the three target group. Mia, a colleague student represents the explorers. She wants to kill time in weekend, so she can use our app to find a exhibition she likes. Zhu Lin, a mom, represents the discoverer. She want to find a kid-friendly exhibition so she can use our app to find a exhbition to go with her son. James Chen represents the validators. As a professinal artist, he wants to find a specific exhibition, and he can use our app to find the one he likes.

User Flow

Because MUSE wants users to find exhibitions accurately and quicky, our user flow is simple but well-designed. We sorted our function and steps trying to provide the best user experience.
